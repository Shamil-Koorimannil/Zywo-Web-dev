Know The Founder

At just 21, Rayan Hany is a passionate Creative Director and Brand Strategist from Kerala, India. He loves turning ideas into visuals that people can connect with. With a degree in Computer Applications and a diploma in Advertising and Photography from the Evoka School of Advertising, he brings a special mix of tech skills and creative energy. This makes him one of the best new leaders in helping brands tell their story through great design.

The idea for Zywo came from a simple observation. In 2024, at a business conference, Rayan met many startup founders from across India. They were brilliant and passionate, but their branding didn't show it. Their amazing ideas were hidden behind boring logos and advertisements that didn't work. As a graphic designer, Rayan knew he could help. He believed that great ideas deserve to look great, too.

Inspired, Rayan launched Zywo to help these amazing businesses look as good on the outside as they were on the inside. He wanted to build an agency that would be a true partner to its clients, not just a service for hire. At Zywo, every project is handled with care, blending smart thinking with creative design. For Rayan, it's simple: every little detail matters.

Today, as Zywo's Creative Director and head Brand Strategist, Rayan leads a talented team to help brands tell their stories better. He is a hands-on leader and visualizer, always involved in creating effective online advertisements and marketing solutions. His main goal is to help good businesses get noticed, grow, and build a lasting name for themselves in today's busy world.
