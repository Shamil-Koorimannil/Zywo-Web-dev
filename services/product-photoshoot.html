Product Photoshoot

Photography  Process

Pre-Shoot Planning & Creative Direction

We begin with understanding your brand and goals. Whether for e-commerce, social media, or catalog, the shoot is planned around the purpose, platform, and persona.

What we do:
Understand your product line, industry, and visual tone
Determine shoot purpose: catalog, Amazon, social, brand campaign, etc.
Finalize style: white background, lifestyle, flat lay, macro/detail, model-based
Build shot list with angles, grouping, features, and styling notes
Prepare props, moodboards, and reference frames

Deliverables:
Creative brief and visual direction deck
Moodboard with styling references
Confirmed shot list (with number of SKUs and frame types)

Setup & Styling

Every product shoot is only as strong as its setup. We style, prep, and place each item with detail and consistency to align with your brand standards.

What we do:
Product unboxing, cleaning, preparation
Light and background setup (white, matte, lifestyle set, etc.)
Prop integration (if required)
Product styling (folds, angles, lighting highlights)
Test lighting & composition

Deliverables:
Setup previews for client approval (on request)
Styled frame test shots (optional pre-approval)

Photoshoot & Execution

This is where we capture the magic. Our photographers capture multiple angles, compositions, and variations per product — for both web and print versatility.

What we do:
Studio or on-location photography
Multiple frame types: hero shot, detail, group, 360Â°, usage, scale
Maintain product consistency in angle, crop, and light
Real-time preview review (on request)

Deliverables:
High-resolution RAW files (internal)
Final selected shots for editing

Retouching & Image Optimization

We professionally retouch every image to ensure clarity, color accuracy, and consistency — ready for any screen or print format.

What we do:
Background removal or cleaning (white/transparent)
Color correction and exposure balancing
Dust, reflection, and blemish removal
Cropping and resizing for platforms
Export in e-commerce optimized formats

Deliverables:
High-res JPEGs (print-ready)
Web-optimized versions (compressed, sharp)
Transparent PNGs (if needed)
PSDs or layered files (optional)

Delivery & Licensing

We wrap up the process with smooth file handoff and usage rights documentation.

What we do:
Organize and label folders by product/SKU/use-case
Deliver via Google Drive, Dropbox, or client-preferred platform
Provide commercial usage rights and license (as per agreement)

Deliverables:
Final images in multiple resolutions
Thumbnail previews for quick selection
Usage rights document
Optional metadata/alt text sheet for SEO uploads
