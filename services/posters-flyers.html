Poster & Flyer

Briefing & Objective Understanding

Every effective poster or flyer starts with a clear goal — whether it's to inform, promote, invite, or sell. We begin by understanding what success looks like for your campaign.

What we do:
Creative briefing call or form
Define poster/flyer purpose (event, promo, sale, awareness, etc.)
Identify target audience and call-to-action
Determine distribution type: print, digital, or both

Deliverables:
Creative brief document
Targeted content plan (copy + visuals)
Format recommendation (A4, A5, DL, square, etc.)

Content & Copy Development

We help write or organize your content so the message is strong, clear, and hierarchy-driven — ideal for quick scanning and retention.

What we do:
Headline & subheading creation
Promotional copywriting or refining
Hierarchy and message flow planning
CTA development

Deliverables:
Approved headline and body text
Optional tagline/slogan
Visual structure for layout (text + image balance)

Visual Design & Layout

We translate your message into a design that's bold, clean, and on-brand — customized for its environment, whether it's on a wall, in hand, or online.

What we do:
Layout design with typography, color, and spacing
Image/iconography sourcing or custom creation
Integration of logos, QR codes, social handles, or maps
Visual hierarchy for readability from near and far

Deliverables:
2-3 design concepts (first round)
High-resolution design previews/mockups
Poster/Flyer source file (AI, PSD, or InDesign)

Review & Refinement

We work closely with you to fine-tune the design through a collaborative review process.

What we do:
Present initial options
Collect stakeholder feedback
Apply design revisions
Finalize layout, alignment, and formatting

Deliverables:
Revised final design
Preview mockups in context (wall frame, social post, etc.)
Final sign-off for print/export

Final Delivery (Print & Digital)

We prepare your file in all formats and resolutions needed — whether for digital distribution, offset printing, or sharing on social media.

What we do:
Export in print-ready format (CMYK with bleeds)
Export for digital/web use (RGB, optimized sizes)
Provide guidance on print specs (paper type, size, finishes)

Deliverables:
Print-ready PDF with crop marks and bleed
Web-optimized JPG/PNG (for WhatsApp, Instagram, email, etc.)
Editable source files (optional)
Thumbnail or story-size version (on request)
