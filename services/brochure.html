Brochure Design

Brochure  Process

Requirement Discovery & Content Mapping

We begin by understanding your business, goals, and the message you want to convey. We also analyze your target audience and the format that best suits your communication needs.

What we do:
Client briefing session
Competitor analysis (if needed)
Determine brochure type (bi-fold, tri-fold, multi-page, digital PDF, etc.)
Organize content structure and flow

Deliverables:
Creative brief
Content outline or wireframe
Suggested brochure format & page plan

Content Development (Optional)

If you don't already have brochure content, we help write it — structured, persuasive, and aligned with your tone of voice.

What we do:
Copywriting for headings, sections, and CTAs
Proofreading existing content
Visual-to-text flow balance
Language localization (if needed)

Deliverables:
Finalized brochure copy
Editable text document
Optional tagline or brand messaging suggestions

Visual Design & Layout

We craft a visual story that's clean, brand-aligned, and conversion-driven. Every layout is custom-designed — no generic templates here.

What we do:
Layout design (based on selected format)
Color palette, typography, and grid system
Image and icon integration
Alignment with your brand identity

Deliverables:
2-3 initial layout concepts
High-fidelity brochure design in Figma, InDesign, or Illustrator
Realistic mockups (print and digital preview)

Review & Feedback

We involve you throughout the process to ensure the design meets your expectations and business objectives.

What we do:
Present design for feedback
Refine design based on revisions
Final approval with checklist

Deliverables:
Revised design file
Mockup visuals for sharing/presentation
Final sign-off document (optional)

Final Delivery & Print/Digital Handoff

Once approved, we export your brochure for the final use — whether print-ready or optimized for digital sharing.

What we do:
Prepare files with bleeds, crop marks, and CMYK for print
Create compressed PDFs for digital sharing
Assist with printer coordination (if required)
Deliver source files for future edits

Deliverables:
Print-ready files (PDF + CMYK)
Digital version (PDF + RGB/web)
Editable source file (AI/PSD/INDD)
Optional: Interactive PDF with links (for digital use)
