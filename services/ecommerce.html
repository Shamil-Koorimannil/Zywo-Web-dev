E-commerce  Process

Account Creation & Platform Setup

We start by selecting the right platform based on your business goals — and handle all the backend setup required to get you selling online.

What we do:
Platform consultation (Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)
Seller account registration & KYC verification
GST, bank details, shipping setup
Policy, return, and payment configuration

Deliverables:
Fully verified seller account
Account credentials + platform documentation
Store dashboard access with initial walkthrough

Store Branding & Optimization

We make your store look professional and trustworthy, aligning it with your brand's visual identity.

What we do:
Logo and store banner design
Store description & brand story writing
Storefront layout customization (for Shopify/WooCommerce)
Review & rating section setup

Deliverables:
Branded storefront assets (logo, banners, thumbnails)
Brand bio + SEO-optimized descriptions
Store layout preview (where applicable)

Product Listing Creation

We help your products stand out with high-quality listings that convert — optimized for both users and platform search algorithms.

What we do:
Product photography (if needed)
Product title & description writing
Category mapping, tags & filters setup
SKU, pricing, inventory setup
Bulk upload via CSV/Excel (if needed)

Deliverables:
Individual or bulk product listings
Optimized product titles + bullet points
High-quality thumbnails (with white background if needed)
Upload-ready product sheet (Excel/CSV)

Store Management & Inventory Handling

Once you're live, we help manage the day-to-day operations so you can focus on growing the business.

What we do:
Order tracking & fulfillment monitoring
Inventory status updates
Returns and refund handling support
Promotions, deals, and offer setup

Deliverables:
Monthly inventory report
Order dashboard access
Setup of promotional campaigns (if needed)
Support ticket handling guide (if required)

Analytics & Performance Reporting

We track performance and provide insights on how to improve conversions, rankings, and revenue.

What we do:
Traffic and sales report analysis
Product performance breakdown
Customer behavior insights
Recommendations for improvement

Deliverables:
Monthly performance report
Sales and traffic charts
Actionable insights & strategy update
