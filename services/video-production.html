Video Production

Video Production  Process

Strategy & Concept Development

We begin by understanding your brand, goals, and audience — and develop a creative concept and video format that aligns with your campaign objective.

What we do:
Creative briefing & brand voice analysis
Audience & platform targeting (Reels, YouTube, Ads, Website, etc.)
Define video type: promo, explainer, testimonial, ad, product showcase, etc.
Concept development & scripting

Deliverables:
Creative concept document
Script or storyline
Moodboard or sample references
Shot list or animation flow (if applicable)

Pre-Production Planning

We lay the groundwork for production — including visual planning, scheduling, casting, and all logistics to ensure smooth execution.

What we do:
Storyboarding & scene breakdown
Location scouting or scene setup
Talent sourcing (voiceover, actors — if needed)
Props, wardrobe, set prep (if applicable)
Schedule planning

Deliverables:
Final storyboard / shot plan
Location and cast list (if used)
Production schedule
Final script approval

Production / Filming (or Animation)

This is where ideas come alive. We shoot or animate the video with professional quality visuals, audio, and lighting.

For Live Action:
On-location or in-studio filming
Camera setup, lighting, direction, and audio capture
Voiceover recording and direction

For Motion Graphics / Animation:
Scene-by-scene animation
Text overlays, visual storytelling
Sync with voice and background music

Deliverables:
Raw footage (optional)
Behind-the-scenes stills (optional)
Animation draft (if animated)

Post-Production & Editing

We polish the visuals, add music, enhance audio, and ensure the video is platform-ready in all formats.

What we do:
Video editing (cutting, pacing, transitions)
Color grading and correction
Background music and SFX integration
Subtitles/captions (if needed)
Logo animations / intros / outros
Voice sync and sound mixing

Deliverables:
First cut for review
Final video in requested resolution and formats (MP4, MOV, etc.)
Platform-optimized exports (e.g., Reels, YouTube, Stories, 16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
Source project files (optional)

Revisions & Delivery

We include feedback rounds to ensure the video meets expectations and fits your vision before final handoff.

What we do:
Present draft version(s)
Collect and implement feedback
Final approval
Handover of media files & usage guidelines

Deliverables:
Final high-quality video (in multiple formats if needed)
Compressed version for web/mobile
Thumbnail & preview stills
Video usage guidelines (platform placement, hashtags, etc.)

Types of Videos We Create

Brand introduction videos
Product explainers & demos
Instagram Reels & YouTube Shorts
Corporate interviews & testimonials
Event promos & highlight reels
Animated motion graphics / typography videos
Ad creatives for Meta, YouTube, Google, and more
