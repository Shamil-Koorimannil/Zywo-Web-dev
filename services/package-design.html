Package Design

Package  Process

Product & Market Discovery

We start by understanding the product, its audience, and the shelf it will live on. This ensures the packaging speaks clearly and stands out in the right context.

What we do:
Product and use-case understanding
Target market and consumer behavior analysis
Competitor packaging research
Packaging goals: function, emotion, shelf impact

Deliverables:
Discovery brief
Moodboard for look & feel
Packaging creative direction (tone, visual keywords)

Structural & Material Planning

Before design begins, we define the physical parameters — structure, size, folds, dielines, and materials — aligned with your budget and distribution needs.

What we do:
Define packaging type (box, pouch, bottle, etc.)
Review dielines or create custom layout
Material, finish, and sustainability planning
Printer/production collaboration (if applicable)

Deliverables:
Finalized dieline template (or sourced from printer)
Structural mockups or renders
Material and finish recommendations

Visual Design & Branding

We bring your packaging to life — integrating your brand identity into a layout that informs, excites, and persuades on-shelf or on-screen.

What we do:
Full front/back/side package design
Typography, color, and iconography alignment
Labeling & regulatory compliance (e.g. barcode, ingredients, nutrition panel)
Differentiated SKUs/variants (if required)

Deliverables:
Complete package design (front + sides + back)
2-3 design concepts (initial stage)
Mockups and visuals for presentation
Editable source files (AI/PSD)

Review & Refinement

We collaborate with you through feedback rounds to refine the design until it's final — both creatively and technically.

What we do:
Present design concepts
Collect stakeholder feedback
Apply design and layout refinements
Prepare for prepress review

Deliverables:
Revised design files (print-ready)
Packaging preview mockups
Final approval checklist

Finalization & Production Handoff

We prepare everything for printing or digital rollout, coordinating with your vendor to ensure flawless execution.

What we do:
Finalize files for print (CMYK color space, bleeds, crop marks, etc.)
Liaise with your printer (if required)
Provide production-ready assets

Deliverables:
Print-ready PDF with specifications
Editable source files (if requested)
Production guide with material and color notes
Optional: 3D rendering or animation of final packaging
