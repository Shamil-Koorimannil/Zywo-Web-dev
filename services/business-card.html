Business Card

Business Card  Process

Brand Understanding & Creative Direction

We start by understanding your brand's tone, audience, and industry — ensuring the design reflects your personality while maintaining professionalism.

What we do:
Client briefing session
Review of existing brand assets (logo, colors, fonts)
Analyze industry and competitor card trends
Decide on card orientation (horizontal/vertical), size, and print type

Deliverables:
Creative brief summary
Moodboard or direction reference (optional)
Specification document (material, finish, size)

Layout & Design Exploration

We design clean and impactful layouts that balance visual appeal with essential information — customized for single or double-sided use.

What we do:
Design front and back layout options
Align typography and color with brand identity
Integrate logo, name, role, and contact details clearly
Design for minimalism, elegance, or bold impact (based on brand tone)

Deliverables:
2-3 initial design options
Variations with different layouts or fonts
Digital preview/mockup on realistic card background

Print Optimization & Production Support

We ensure that your design is technically perfect for print — with all specs considered for color, bleed, and resolution.

What we do:
CMYK color conversion for accurate printing
Bleed and trim mark setup
Paper, finish, and coating consultation (matte, glossy, textured, etc.)
Spot UV, embossing, or foil design (optional)

Deliverables:
Print-ready files (PDF, AI, or EPS with bleed & crop marks)
Production guide for your printer
Optional: Editable source file (AI or PSD)

Digital Business Card Option (Optional)

Need to go paperless? We can convert your card into a digital business card (vCard or scannable format) for mobile sharing.

What we do:
Design digital-friendly version (mobile cropped)
Export as image or PDF with QR code
Optional: Setup of NFC-enabled card design

Deliverables:
Digital card file (JPG/PNG/PDF)
QR code image (linking to portfolio, site, or contact sheet)
Optional vCard (.vcf) format

Card Types We Design

Minimalist professional cards
Bold creative agency cards
Textured, embossed or foil-stamped designs
Transparent or die-cut cards
Digital/NFC-based smart cards
