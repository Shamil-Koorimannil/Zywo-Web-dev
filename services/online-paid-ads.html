Online Paid Ads

Paid Ads  Process

Discovery & Campaign Goal Mapping

We begin by understanding your business objectives and defining the right campaign strategy for your brand and budget.

What we do:
Understand your offer, product/service, audience & sales funnel
Define ad goals: brand awareness, leads, conversions, traffic, app installs, etc.
Choose platforms: Meta (Facebook, Instagram), Google Ads, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.
Determine budget allocation & campaign timelines

Deliverables:
Ad campaign brief
Media plan & budget roadmap
Target audience persona (demographics, interests, behavior)

Ad Strategy & Copy + Creative Planning

We craft scroll-stopping ad creatives and messaging that trigger curiosity, trust, and clicks — tailored for every platform and audience segment.

What we do:
Write multiple ad copy variations (headline, primary text, CTA)
Design static visuals, motion graphics, carousel cards, video reels, etc.
A/B creative testing strategy
Align message with user stage (cold, warm, hot traffic)

Deliverables:
Campaign structure plan (e.g., TOFU-MOFU-BOFU funnel)
Ad copy deck
Ad creative pack (images, videos, carousels)
Preview mockups

Campaign Setup & Technical Configuration

We configure your ad accounts, set up tracking, and launch campaigns with precision across platforms.

What we do:
Meta Ads Manager / Google Ads account setup
Pixel, event, and conversion tracking (via Google Tag Manager)
Custom audience & lookalike setup
Campaign, ad set, and ad creation in manager
Geo, device, and interest targeting

Deliverables:
Live campaign setup (with access)
Conversion tracking report (events, goals, URLs)
Pixel/Tag Manager integration
Dashboard access (Meta or Google)

Monitoring, Optimization & Scaling

We don't set and forget. We continuously monitor ad performance and refine targeting, creatives, and bidding to maximize ROI.

What we do:
Daily/weekly ad performance monitoring
Optimize ads based on CTR, CPC, ROAS, conversions, etc.
Kill underperformers, boost high-performers
Scale budgets strategically

Deliverables:
Weekly performance snapshot
Ad optimization log (what changed and why)
ROAS and conversion insights
Scaling strategy (when ready)

Reporting & Insights

We provide transparent, actionable reports with real business insights — not just numbers.

What we do:
Monthly ad performance report
Analyze ROI, lead quality, bounce rate, audience engagement
Insights on creative performance
Strategic recommendations for future campaigns

Deliverables:
Visual report (Google Looker Studio, PDF or Notion)
Audience behavior insights
Learnings + next-step recommendations

LinkedIn Ads (for B2B)
Twitter/X Ads (on request)
Pinterest Ads (for D2C brands)
Snapchat Ads / TikTok Ads (for youth-oriented content)
