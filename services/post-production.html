Post Production

Post-Production Process

Intake & Asset Collection

Every edit starts with understanding your project's vision and receiving all necessary assets — from footage to audio, brand elements, and references.

What we do:
Briefing session or creative form
Collect raw footage, audio files, brand kits, and references
Review visual style, tone, and video duration requirements
Define export platforms (Instagram, YouTube, website, ads, etc.)

Deliverables:
Creative editing brief
File intake checklist
Project folder setup (organized by media, audio, graphics, etc.)

Video Editing & Narrative Assembly

We organize, trim, and craft a visual story with rhythm and purpose — balancing emotion, energy, and clarity.

What we do:
Multi-camera syncing (if applicable)
Selection of best takes and scenes
Rough cut creation based on storyboard or script
Pacing, rhythm, and transitions
Add titles, lower-thirds, text overlays, or subtitles

Deliverables:
First-cut or rough edit (for review)
Narrative or highlight timeline
Feedback-ready version (with time stamps)

Color Grading & Visual Enhancement

We elevate the visual tone with pro-level color correction and grading that fits your brand and mood.

What we do:
Color correction (white balance, exposure, contrast)
Artistic color grading (warm, cinematic, natural, vibrant, etc.)
Match lighting across shots for consistency
Apply LUTs if specified

Deliverables:
Final graded version
Still frame previews (for client review or thumbnails)

Sound Design & Audio Mastering

Audio is half the story — we ensure your sound is crisp, balanced, and engaging.

What we do:
Background music sync & mood matching
Voice-over syncing and editing
Sound effects design (if needed)
Audio leveling, EQ, noise reduction
Final mastering for platform volume standards

Deliverables:
Mixed and mastered audio
Multiple audio mixdowns (if required)
Separate voice/music stems (on request)

Final Cut & Export

We format your video to fit every screen — from vertical Reels to wide-screen presentations and everything in between.

What we do:
Add intro/outro animations or branded elements
Platform-specific exports (16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, etc.)
Compression and optimization without quality loss
Deliver in multiple resolutions: Full HD, 4K, mobile, web

Deliverables:
Final video file (MP4, MOV, or client-preferred format)
Social-ready versions (e.g., Reels, Shorts, Story)
Thumbnail image(s)
Editable project files (Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci, etc. - optional)

Special Editing Services We Offer

Instagram Reels / TikTok style fast-cut edits
Corporate & testimonial edits
Cinematic travel or event montages
YouTube intros & long-form editing
Commercial and ad film editing
Motion graphic transitions & text animation
Vlogs, music videos, and product reels
