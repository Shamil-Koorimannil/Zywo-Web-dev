Irumb Karimnaakum

Coolcane  Campaign

Campaign Background

This campaign was built for Coolcane, a brand moving local street sugarcane juice into a hygienic, premium offering neatly sealed inside uniquely molded sugarcane-shaped bottles.

The Challenge

We needed to profoundly convey the unadulterated sweetness of the brand. We constructed the concept that Coolcane is so intensely sweet it has the power to turn even the most 'iron-hearted' people into complete sweethearts.

Execution & Success

Anchored by the tagline "Irumb Karimnaakum" (Even iron will turn to sugar cane), we launched creative visual posters showing notorious arch-enemies happily sharing and drinking Coolcane together. The campaign perfectly captured the brand’s positioning and elicited an overwhelmingly sweet response from the public.
