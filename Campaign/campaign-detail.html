Manjayellam Mouzy yalla

Mouzy  Avil Milk

Campaign Background

This campaign was designed for Mouzy Avil Milk, a highly popular brand operating numerous outlets inside and outside India. The initiative was born to combat the sudden surge of copycat shops that sprung up in huge numbers, threatening the brand's reputation.

The Challenge

The hallmark of any Mouzy outlet is its vibrant yellow interior and exterior. Counterfeiters took advantage of this signature trait, painting their own shops in the exact same yellow aesthetic. People unknowingly visited these imitation shops and tasted inferior avil milk. Believing it was the original Mouzy, they directed their dissatisfaction at the brand.

Execution & Success

To solve the brand-identity crisis, we launched an aggressive awareness campaign with the catchy tagline "Manjayellam Mouzy yalla" (Every yellow is not Mouzy). Together with beautifully designed creatives, this slogan resonated quickly with the masses and became a huge success in helping people differentiate the true brand from impostors.
