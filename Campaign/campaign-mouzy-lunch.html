Mouzy  Avil Milk

Campaign Background

This campaign was launched for Mouzy Avil Milk to highlight its versatility as a fulfilling and refreshing daytime meal alternative. It was designed to capture the attention of students and busy professionals on the go.

The Challenge

Many people look for a quick, exceptionally affordable, and tasty option for lunch. The core idea was to cleverly position Mouzy Avil Milk as the perfect minimal replacement for a traditional heavy lunch, ensuring nobody has to compromise on taste or budget.

Execution & Success

Armed with the highly relatable tagline "Mouzy yullappol oonenthin?" (Why have meals when you have Mouzy?), the campaign successfully tapped into the quick-lunch crowd. People began actively grabbing Avil Milk as a proper alternative to their heavy lunch breaks.
